Step-by-step flow

  • Step 1. Choose the search route that matches your details

    Start with the official route that matches the information you already have, such as family details or Aadhaar-linked details.

  • Step 2. Verify the details carefully on the official screen

    Check the name, state, district, or family details before you search so that you do not create a false mismatch.

  • Step 3. Move to the next page only after you see a result

    If the record appears, continue to the download or status path. If it does not, review eligibility or document readiness first.
