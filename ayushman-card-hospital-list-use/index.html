Step-by-step flow

  • Step 1. Start with the official hospital search

    Use the official PMJAY hospital search to confirm that the hospital is listed before you rely on it.

  • Step 2. Verify acceptance and treatment context with the hospital

    A listed hospital may still require you to confirm package fit, availability, or acceptance at the hospital level.

  • Step 3. Move to the card-use guide after hospital verification

    Once the hospital route is clear, continue to the practical card-use page for the next step.

Keep in mind

Common mistake

Assuming any hospital blog list is the same as the official empanelled list.

Good habit

Confirm treatment fit and acceptance with the hospital after you verify the official listing.
